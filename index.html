About Us

ASE Morningstar is a faith-rooted sanctuary offering love, education, food, medical care, and guidance to children formerly on the streets. We believe in dignity, healing, and hope through structured support.

Our History

Since 1993, Morningstar has been led by the Assumption Sisters of Eldoret. Through education and holistic care, we‚Äôve shaped lives and restored dignity in the hearts of those abandoned by society.

Education Support
